Juriclips and others publications Juriclips

Each of our professional committees produces three times per year an electronic legal information newsletter called Juriclip. Available in 16 different fields of law, the Juriclip is designed to provide valuable advice and keep readers informed of recent developments and current events in specific areas of expertise or sectors of activity. Most of these newsletters are in French only.
