News COVID-19 - NOTARIAL SERVICES

News: Published On June 2 2020

In accordance with government instructions and in order to prevent the spread of Covid-19 and to contribute to the well-being and health of our colleagues and our community, our professionals are currently teleworking and in position to respond to your needs.

We confirm that all our notaries are at your service to accompany you in your personal and professional business.

Due to the state of emergency, the Minister of Health and Social Services authorized the Chambre des notaires du Qu√©bec to establish a procedure under which a notary may close notarial acts en minutes on remote technology, since April 1, 2020.

The notaries in our firm received training on this new procedure and are now able to meet all your needs remotely.

In order to receive this service, you will need the following tools: a computer or a tablet, a microphone, a camera, a cellular or landline telephone, a personal email address, an internet connection, and the Teams application.

Our notaries will support you throughout the process, particularly in terms of the installation and use of the required application.

You can also communicate with our team quickly and easily. To find the contact information of our notaries in your area, visit our website at www.cainlamarre.ca.
